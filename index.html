About Me

Hello everyone, I am a Female AstroGrapher from China. I am not only interested in photography, but also very fond of astronomy. Because of the bad weather, I have processed lots of data acquired by astrolovers everywhere. I hope you like these photos. ：）
Last year, I set up my own remote observatory in Dabancheng, Xinjiang, China, but there're always some unresolved problems. In the past year, my remote observatory was rarely used. I hope I can use it more in the future.
